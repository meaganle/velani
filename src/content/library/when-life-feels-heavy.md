Some seasons feel heavier than others.

Not dramatic. Not falling apart. Just… heavier. More tired than usual. More emotional. More scattered. The smallest things suddenly feel harder than they should.

And often, our first instinct is to push harder. Be more disciplined. Fix the routine. Try to get back on track.

But sometimes life isn't asking us to push. Sometimes it's asking us to soften. To pause long enough to notice: *of course this feels hard right now.*

Maybe you're carrying more. Maybe energy is lower. Maybe life quietly changed and you haven't adjusted yet.

Not every heavy season needs fixing. Some simply need gentler expectations. Smaller goals. More support. Less pressure.

When life feels heavy, maybe today is not the day for reinvention. Maybe today is: drink water. Answer one important email. Take the walk. Rest when you can. Choose one meaningful move and let that be enough.

You do not need to carry everything perfectly. You are allowed to move slowly. And slowly still counts.

---

**If today feels heavier than usual, ask yourself:**

*What would make today feel 10% lighter?*
